Chill

Alchemist Bar

Nairobi, Kenya
Nairobi's creative scene distilled into one shipping container complex. Art on the walls (it rotates monthly). DJ booths in converted containers pumping everything from Amapiano to house to afrobeats. Craft cocktails made by bartenders who actually care. Street food stalls offering tacos, pizza, and nyama choma. The crowd ranges from photographers to tech founders to fashion designers to people who just want good music and don't take themselves too seriously. Dress however you want — there's no code. It starts slow on a Friday. By midnight it's full. By 2am it's one of those rare nights where everything just works — the music, the people, the energy. Saturday is similar but louder. Sunday is recovery brunch. The kind of place that makes you understand why Nairobi's nightlife has a reputation. Come with friends. Leave with stories.
Best time Friday and Saturday nights
Vibe Art, cocktails, live music, street food
Crowd Photographers, tech founders, creatives
Hours Thursday–Sunday, evening onwards
