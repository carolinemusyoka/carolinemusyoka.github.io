--- layout: place title: "Lala Galu" permalink: /places/lala-galu/ place_category: stay location: "Diani, Kenya" rating: 4 price: "$$$" image: "https://ik.imagekit.io/ngmub3qdz/tr:w-1400,f-auto,q-80/potw/POTW/20250803_173728.jpg" related_article: "/shades-of-blue-down-south/" info: - icon: "map" label: "Location" value: "Adjacent to Boxo Diani" - icon: "tag" label: "Price range" value: "KES 10,000–18,000 per night" - icon: "users" label: "Best for" value: "Couples, families, quiet stays" - icon: "book" label: "How to book" value: "Lala Galu website" --- Sister property to Boxo, same management, same stretch of beach — but the energy is completely different. Where Boxo is pool parties and DJs, Lala Galu is afternoon naps and the sound of waves. The rooms are more traditional. Think whitewashed walls, Swahili-style furniture, and private balconies that look out over the garden towards the ocean. It attracts a slightly older crowd and families who want the beach without the bass. The restaurant is shared with Boxo, which means you get the best of both worlds — Sunnyside Up's food without the noise. Walking between the two properties takes about two minutes down a sandy path, so you can always visit for the vibe and retreat for the peace. Perfect for anyone who loves the coast but needs sleep.
