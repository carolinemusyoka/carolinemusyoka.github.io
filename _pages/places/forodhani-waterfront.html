--- layout: place title: "Forodhani Waterfront" permalink: /places/forodhani-waterfront/ place_category: chill location: "Stone Town, Zanzibar" rating: 5 price: "" image: "https://ik.imagekit.io/ngmub3qdz/tr:w-1400,f-auto,q-80/cities/stone-town/waterfront-sunset.jpg" related_article: "/24-hours-in-stone-town/" info: - icon: "sun" label: "Best time" value: "5:30 PM onwards" - icon: "waves" label: "Vibe" value: "Sunset, people-watching, zero agenda" - icon: "tag" label: "Cost" value: "Free" - icon: "camera" label: "Photo tip" value: "Dhow silhouettes against sunset" --- Find a spot on the seawall. Buy a sugar cane juice from a vendor for a few hundred shillings. Watch the sun set behind dhow silhouettes on the Indian Ocean. That's it. That's the whole activity. And it's one of the best hours you'll spend in East Africa. The light goes from gold to pink to deep orange and the whole town seems to pause. The muezzin calls echo across the rooftops. Children play on the grass behind you. The vendors start firing up their grills for the night market. The day exhales. You don't need a plan here. You don't need a reservation or a ticket or a guide. Just a spot on the wall, something cold to drink, and the willingness to sit still while one of the world's great sunsets does its thing. Free. Effortless. Perfect.
