--- layout: place title: "Kanunga Lakeside Resort" permalink: /places/kanunga-lakeside-resort/ place_category: stay location: "Kanunga, Kenya" rating: 3 price: "$$" image: "https://ik.imagekit.io/ngmub3qdz/tr:w-1400,f-auto,q-80/potw/POTW/20250803_131322.jpg" related_article: "/exploring-kanunga/" info: - icon: "map" label: "Location" value: "Near Ndakaini Dam, Gatundu" - icon: "tag" label: "Price range" value: "KES 3,000–6,000 per night" - icon: "users" label: "Best for" value: "Day trips, quiet weekends" - icon: "book" label: "How to book" value: "Call ahead or walk-in" --- A small, no-frills resort near Ndakaini Dam that quietly punches above its weight. The gardens are well-kept and green, the rooms are clean if basic, and the view over the dam makes up for anything else the place might lack. It's the kind of spot weekend day-trippers from Nairobi come for lunch — a plate of stew and chapati on the terrace overlooking water that shifts from emerald to teal depending on the light. But staying overnight is the move. You get the misty morning views that everyone else drives away from. The highlands air is cool and clean, and the silence at dawn is the kind that recharges something deep. Don't come expecting luxury. Come expecting peace.
